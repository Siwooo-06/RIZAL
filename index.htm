Historical Diorama Project

This website presents a collection of historical dioramas that depict significant moments in Philippine history. Each scene captures emotion, sacrifice, and patriotism.

Execution of Gomburza

The execution of Fathers Gomez, Burgos, and Zamora in 1872 became a symbol of injustice under Spanish rule. Their martyrdom awakened Filipino nationalism and inspired future revolutionaries including Jose Rizal.

Rizal’s Ateneo Days

Rizal excelled during his years at Ateneo Municipal de Manila. His academic excellence and discipline shaped his intellectual growth and prepared him for his role as a reformist and writer.

Doña Lolay’s 50km Walk

Doña Teodora Alonzo walked nearly 50 kilometers to visit her imprisoned son. Her courage and devotion reflect the deep love and sacrifice of a mother during colonial oppression.
